Blackjack

How to play

The purpose of blackjack is to get a higher card count than the dealer, while remaining at or under 21.

Going over 21 results in a bust, and hitting 21 exactly is a blackjack.

Press the 'hit' button to take another card, and the 'stand' button to keep what you have.

The dealer will begin with one card, and will always hit at 16, and will always stand at 17.

There is no betting in this version, and some rules such as splitting cards are removed.

There is only room for 9 cards in a hand. If you get 9 cards, you will automatically stand.

If you tie the dealer, neither a win nor loss is counted.

Aces automatically count for 11, and if you go bust with one in your hand it automatically changes to 1.
